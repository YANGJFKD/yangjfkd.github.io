Jingfeng Yang

I am currently a scientist in Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR). Prior to this, I earned my PhD degree in Computer Science from Singapore Management University (SMU), and both Master and Bachelor degree in Transportation Engineer from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU).

I was fortunate to be advised Prof. Jiangang Jin in my Master study, who led me to the world of research.

My research interests lie in the intersection of deep learning and operations research, with a current focus on the vehicle routing problems.
